# God-Slayer: Rise from Ashes — AI Art Prompts

Six prompts for generating game artwork using Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL·E.
Style guide: Oil painting, dark fantasy, cinematic lighting, 8K, intricate detail.

---

## 1. BASTION — City of Ruins

```
A crumbling fortress city at dusk, gothic stone towers wrapped in dark ivy, 
broken stained glass windows glowing with dying amber embers, 
ash falling like snow, a lone soldier standing at the gate 
looking toward a bloodred horizon, 
oil painting, dark fantasy, 8K, cinematic, highly detailed, 
dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, wide angle, fog rolling through cobblestone streets
```

---

## 2. IGLICZE — Dead Library City

```
A spectral city built among towering black needle spires, 
ancient floating libraries made of bone and stone, 
ghostly blue lanterns illuminating shelves of forbidden tomes, 
a hooded ghost scholar reading a crumbling book, 
moonlit, ethereal fog, oil painting, dark fantasy, 
8K resolution, deep perspective, silver and indigo palette, cinematic
```

---

## 3. CYTADELA — Divine Forge Citadel

```
A colossal divine citadel atop obsidian cliffs, 
molten divine fire pouring from forge chimneys into the abyss, 
a blacksmith god wielding a hammer of pure starlight, 
golden runes etched into black stone walls, 
ruins of slain deities as architecture, 
oil painting, dark epic fantasy, 8K, ultra-detailed, volcanic dramatic lighting
```

## 4. MAIN CHARACTER — The God-Slayer

```
A battle-scarred warrior in shattered divine armor, 
standing in rain over the defeated body of a giant god, 
amber eyes glowing behind a cracked helmet visor, 
a sword radiating dark gold light, cracks of light across dark skin, 
exhausted but triumphant, ash swirling, 
oil painting, dramatic portrait, dark fantasy, 8K, cinematic lighting, intense
```

## 5. BOSS ENCOUNTER — The Last God

```
A titanic celestial god shattering into dark crystal shards, 
its divine form unraveling into void energy, 
a small black-armored warrior slashing through its collapsing chest, 
divine golden light pouring from the wound, 
epic scale, godlike destruction, 
oil painting, dark fantasy, 8K, extreme detail, dramatic top-down angle, awe-inspiring
```

## 6. THE ENDLESS VOID — Final Boss

```
An infinite void entity with a thousand hollow golden eyes 
floating in a collapsing dimension, 
reality shattering like glass around it, 
cosmic horror, the silhouette of a lone warrior below impossibly small, 
tendrils of darkness consuming stars in the background,

```

---

## Usage Tips

- For Midjourney: append `--ar 16:9 --q 2 --stylize 1000` to each prompt  
- For Stable Diffusion: use negative prompt `cartoon, anime, bright colors, low quality, blurry`
- For DALL·E 3: use prompts as-is, they are well-scoped for the style
